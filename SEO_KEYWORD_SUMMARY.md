## Critical Gaps to Address

### Gap 1: PyME Keyword Under-Optimization (CRITICAL)

**Current Status:** 0.4% density (should be 0.6-1.0%)

**Problem:**
- Cumbre IA's primary target market is PyMEs
- Only 4 mentions of "PyME/PyMEs" in entire page
- Missed long-tail keyword opportunity: "IA para PyMEs"

**Business Impact:**
- Missing ~15-25% of potential organic traffic from local PyME searches
- Competitors targeting "PyME Argentina" + "IA" will outrank on these terms

**Solution:** Add strategic PyME mentions:
1. Features section subtitle: "...para que tu PyME alcance su cumbre"
2. Services H2 title: "Servicios de IA para PyMEs"
3. Service card descriptions: Include PyME context
4. Meta description: Add "para PyMEs argentinas"

**Expected ROI:** +15-25% long-tail keyword traffic
